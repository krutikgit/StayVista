DINING

Dine & Imbibe

Discover the eclectic culinary experiences at JW Marriott Maldives Resort & Spa. Start your day with nourishing breakfast at our all-day dining, Aailaa and order your favorite pizza at Fiamma for relaxing lunch by the pool or spice up your day with authentic Thai cuisine at Kaashi. Witness the serene view of Maldivian sunset at Horizon while having your favorite drinks on hand. Visit our Wine Room for a wine-pairing dining experience or create your own cocktail at Rum Baan. Try our signature-smoked cocktails at Wabi Sabi for pre-dinner drinks before indulging your palate with Japanese fare at Hashi or the finest prime cut meats and crustaceans at our signature restaurant, Shio.



FORK & DRINK

Select from one of the resort’s unique dining destinations for an unforgettable experience. Indulge in a chef crafted menu accompanied by a perfect wine pairing by our sommelier.


DINING SERVICES


Fiamma

Savor the delicious and distinctive flavors of freshly prepared Italian cuisine at our al fresco restaurant next to the pool. Relish popular Italian dishes, such as risotto, pasta and wood-fired pizzas, along with refreshing beverages.

Open for lunch

Dress code: Casual


Hashi

Treat yourself to a selection of traditional Japanese specialties, including sake, as you witness our talented teppanyaki chefs showcase their skills in the Maldives. Our Hashi restaurant features classic Japanese décor amid an overwater location..

Open for dinner

Dress code: Smart Casual


Aailaa

This vibrant restaurant offers delectable all-day dining, including an enticing à la carte menu for lunch and dinner. You can also greet the day in the Maldives with our sumptuous breakfast buffet, complete with live cooking stations.

Open for breakfast, lunch and dinner

Dress code: Casual


Kaashi

Enjoy a unique dining experience at our Thai restaurant, set in a stunning treetop location. Take in a cool breeze and panoramic views of the Indian Ocean while relaxing with freshly prepared Thai cuisine and rum cocktails. Open for lunch daily.

Open for lunch

Dress code: Casual


Wabi Sabi

Situated over the water, Wabi Sabi offers Japanese-inspired and signature smoked cocktails. An extensive wine list and Japanese sakes, teas and whiskies are available.

Open for dinner

Dress code: Smart Casual


Horizon

Enjoy a refreshing tropical drink in the swing bar seats of Horizon. With unsurpassed views over the Laccadive Sea, this is the place to soak in a majestic Maldivian sunset and enjoy live entertainment at night.

Open for lunch and dinner

Dress code: Casual
