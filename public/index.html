RESORT OVERVIEW

Indulge Mind, Body and Spirit in Haven Island


A warm welcome to JW Marriott Maldives Resort & Spa, a tropical island paradise situated on a private island in Shaviyani Atoll where unique and meaningful experiences are crafted and created. Stylish overwater and beach villas offer guests their own private pools and decks with stunning views across the Indian Ocean. Sample the flavors of the world at our international restaurants, including treetop dining, buffet breakfasts, Japanese and authentic Italian restaurants. Discover a wide variety of activities available including snorkeling, scuba diving, jet-skiing, sailing, and our Little Griffins Kid's Club. Enjoy our resort's rejuvenating spa or stay fit with our state-of-the-art gym and fitness classes.




THAKURU SERVICE

At JW Marriott Maldives Resort & Spa, we understand the importance of personalized service. Allow our Thakuru team to assist you in arranging a personalized itinerary during your stay with us. Our Thakurus will be more than happy to recommend customized recreational activities on the island such as spa & wellness activities, private dining arrangements, and dolphin cruise to name a few.



ISLAND TIME

Experience meaningful moments together with family and loved ones with an extra hour of daylight each day during your stay at JW Marriott Maldives Resort & Spa. The resort’s time zone is an hour ahead of the capital, Male’. Maldives Time (GMT +5) - Island Time (GMT +6)



LIBRARY & BUSINESS CENTRE

Our Library & Business Center located at the Arrival Pavilion. Opens 24 hours a day, guests may bring their own laptop to Library or use one of the personal computers available with wired internet connections.
